JEE MAIN

Admission into IIITs, NITs, govt. colleges

  • Exam duration : 3 hours

  • Institutes : IIITs, NITs, CFTIs

  • Exam Type : Offline - Computer based

  • Marks : 300

  • Total Questions : 75 questions
    60 MCQs (compulsory)
    30 Numericals (15 to be attempted)

  • Frequency : Twice a year (Jan & Apr)

  • Subjects : Physics, Chemistry & Maths

  • No. of Attempts : 6 times (3 consecutive years)

  • Total Questions : 150 MCQs
