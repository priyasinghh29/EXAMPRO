UPSC

Recruitment for All India Civil Services, Group 'A' & Group 'B' Civil Services

  • Exam duration : 3 hours (each paper)

  • Exam Type : Offline

  • Marks : 400 - Prelims, 1750 - Mains

  • Total Questions : CSAT - 80, GS - 100 & Mains - 20 (each paper)

  • Frequency : Once a year

  • Subjects : 26 different subjects

  • No. of Attempts : 6 - Gen (till 32 years), 9 - OBC (till 35 years) & SC/ST - Unlimited (till 37 years)

IFoS

Recruitment for the Indian Forest Services

  • Exam duration : Prelim - 2 hours (each), Mains - 3 hours (each)

  • Exam Type : Offline

  • Marks : Prelim - 400 (200 each), Mains - 1400 & Interview - 300

  • Total Questions : Prelim - 100 (I) & 80 (II)

  • Frequency : Once a year (Jun)

  • Subjects : Prelim - General Studies, CSAT
    Mains - General English, GK, and optional subjects

  • No. of Attempts : Gen - 6, OBC - 9 & SC/ST - unlimited (within age limits)

CDS

Recruitment for IMA, INA, AFA & OTA.

  • Exam duration : 2 hours per paper

  • Exam Type : Offline

  • Marks : IMA, INA & AFA - 300 (100 each), OTA - 200 (100 each)

  • Total Questions : IMA, INA & AFA - 300 (100 each), OTA - 200 (100 each)

  • Frequency : Twice a year

  • Subjects : IMA, INA & AFA - English, GK, Elementary Mathematics & OTA - Eng, GK

  • No. of Attempts : No limit (within age limits)

NDA

Admission to the National Defence Academy and Naval Academy

  • Exam duration : 2.5 hours per papers

  • Exam Type : Offline

  • Marks : 900 marks (written exam); 900 marks (SSB interview)

  • Total Questions : Maths - 120 questions, General Ability Test: 150 questions

  • Frequency : Twice a year

  • Subjects : Maths, General Ability Test (includes Eng, GK)

  • No. of Attempts : No limit (within age limits)

CAPF

Recruitment for Assistant Commandants in CAPFs - BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, and SSB

  • Exam duration : Paper I - 2 hours; Paper II - 3 hours

  • Exam Type : Offline

  • Marks : Paper I - 250 marks, Paper II - 200 marks

  • Total Questions : Paper I - 125 questions, Paper II - Descriptive

  • Frequency : Once a year

  • Subjects : Paper I - General Ability and Intelligence, Paper II - General Studies, Essay & Comprehension

  • No. of Attempts : No limit (within age limits)

IES

Recruitment for the Indian Economic Service

  • Exam duration : 3 hours per paper

  • Exam Type : Offline

  • Marks : 1000 marks (written exam), 200 marks (interview)

  • Total Questions : Varies by paper (Descriptive type)

  • Frequency : Once a year

  • Subjects : General English, General Studies, Economics

  • No. of Attempts : Gen - 6, OBC - 9, unlimited - SC/ST (within age limits)
