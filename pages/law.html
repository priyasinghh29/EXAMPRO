CLAT

Admission to LL.B and LL.M law programs in 22 NLUs

  • Exam duration : 2 hours

  • Institutes : NLUs & other participating law schools

  • Exam Type : Offline (pen & paper)

  • Marks : 150

  • Total Questions : 150

  • Frequency : Once a year

  • Subjects : English Language, Current Affairs incl. GK, Legal Reasoning, Logical Reasoning, Quantitative Techniques

  • No. of Attempts : No limit

AILET

Admission to BA LL.B (Hons), LL.M, and Ph.D. programs

  • Exam duration : 1.5 hours

  • Institutes : NLU Delhi

  • Exam Type : Offline (pen & paper)

  • Marks : 150

  • Total Questions : 150

  • Frequency : Once a year

  • Subjects : English, GK, Legal Aptitude, Reasoning, Maths

  • No. of Attempts : No limit

LSAT

Admission to undergrad and postgrad law programs in law schools across India

  • Exam duration : 2 hours 20 minutes

  • Institutes : Various law schools in India

  • Exam Type : Online (CBT)

  • Marks : 420-480

  • Total Questions : Varies (typically around 92-100 questions)

  • Frequency : Twice a year

  • Subjects : Analytical Reasoning, Logical Reasoning (1 and 2), Reading Comprehension

  • No. of Attempts : No limit

DU LLB

Admission to LL.B program in the Faculty of Law, Delhi University

  • Exam duration : 2 hours

  • Institutes : Faculty of Law, DU

  • Exam Type : Online (CBT)

  • Marks : 400

  • Total Questions : 100

  • Frequency : Once a year

  • Subjects : English Language Comprehension, GK, Legal Awareness and Aptitude, Analytical Abilities

  • No. of Attempts : No limit

IPU CET

Admission to BA LL.B and BBA LL.B programs in IPU colleges.

  • Exam duration : 2.5 hours

  • Institutes : Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University

  • Exam Type : Online (CBT)

  • Marks : 400

  • Total Questions : 100

  • Frequency : Once a year

  • Subjects : English Language and Comprehension, GK, Legal Aptitude, Reasoning

  • No. of Attempts : No limit

MHCET Law

Admission to LL.B (5 years) and LL.B (3 years) programs in law colleges across Maharashtra.

  • Exam duration : 2 hours

  • Institutes : Law colleges in Maharashtra

  • Exam Type : Online (CBT)

  • Marks : 150

  • Total Questions : 150

  • Frequency : Once a year

  • Subjects : Legal Aptitude & Legal Reasoning, GK with Current Affairs, Logical and Analytical Reasoning, English, Mathematical Aptitude

  • No. of Attempts : No limit
