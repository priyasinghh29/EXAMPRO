TECHNIQUES

These will help you enhance your learning and productivity

POMODORO TIMER


click me!

The Pomodoro Technique increases productivity by splitting work into 25-minute focused intervals with short breaks. It keeps your mind fresh, reduces distractions, and makes tasks easier.

FEYNMAN TECHNIQUE

  • Pick a topic you want to understand deeply.
  • Explain the concept in simple terms as if teaching a child.
  • Review your explanation and find areas where you struggle..
  • Go back to your study material to understand and simplify the gaps.
  • Refine your explanation until you can teach it effortlessly.

ACTIVE RECALL
  • Study the material.
  • Close your notes and recall as much as possible.
  • Write down what you remember.
  • Check your work against the material.
  • Review missed points and repeat.

SPACED REPETITION
  • Study the content thoroughly.
  • Plan review sessions at increasing intervals (e.g., 1 day, 3 days, 1 week).
  • Actively recall and review the material during each session.
  • Increase the intervals between reviews as you become more confident..
